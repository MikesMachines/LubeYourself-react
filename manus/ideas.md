**Design Movement:** Clinical Modernism with Organic Warmth

**Core Principles:**
1. Scientific credibility through clean, structured layouts that evoke medical journals and research papers
2. Approachable warmth via soft, natural color palettes and organic shapes that counter clinical sterility
3. Educational clarity with generous whitespace, clear typography hierarchies, and digestible information architecture
4. Trust-building transparency through visible ingredient lists, research citations, and honest safety information

**Color Philosophy:** 
A foundation of warm neutrals (cream, soft beige, warm gray) paired with muted botanical accents (sage green, soft terracotta, dusty rose). The palette evokes natural ingredients and body-safe formulations while maintaining professional credibility. Avoid bright, synthetic colors that might suggest artificial ingredients.

**Layout Paradigm:**
Asymmetric grid system with distinct content zones. Left-aligned text blocks paired with right-side visual elements create a research paper aesthetic. Generous margins and padding emphasize the "breathing room" of safety and care. Card-based layouts for products and articles with subtle shadows for depth.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Ingredient "molecule" illustrations - abstract, minimalist representations of chemical structures that are beautiful rather than intimidating
2. Soft-edged content containers with subtle gradients that suggest natural transitions
3. Citation-style reference numbers throughout educational content linking to sources

**Interaction Philosophy:**
Interactions should feel deliberate and informative rather than flashy. Hover states reveal additional information (like ingredient tooltips). Smooth, measured transitions suggest careful consideration. CTAs are clear and honest, never manipulative.

**Animation:**
Gentle fade-ins and subtle scale transforms on scroll. Ingredient cards might have a gentle "float" effect. Navigation transitions are smooth but quick (200-300ms). Avoid bouncy or elastic easing - use ease-out for a more scientific, measured feel.

**Typography System:**
- Display: Fraunces (a serif with warmth and character, suggesting both tradition and approachability)
- Body: Inter (clean, readable, modern sans-serif for scientific credibility)
- Accent/Labels: DM Sans (geometric sans for ingredient lists and technical information)
Hierarchy: Bold display headers (48-64px), medium subheads (24-32px), regular body (16-18px), small labels (14px)

**Design Movement:** Brutalist Honesty meets Sensual Minimalism

**Core Principles:**
1. Radical transparency through exposed structure, raw typography, and unpolished authenticity
2. Sensual simplicity via bold negative space, tactile visual textures, and confident restraint
3. Anti-commercial aesthetic that rejects traditional e-commerce polish in favor of educational directness
4. Functional beauty where every element serves a clear purpose without decoration for decoration's sake

**Color Philosophy:**
Near-monochrome foundation (charcoal black, pure white, concrete gray) with strategic punctuation from a single accent color: a deep, rich burgundy that suggests both blood (body) and wine (pleasure). This limited palette creates stark contrast and forces focus on content hierarchy. The burgundy appears sparingly for critical CTAs and safety warnings, making it impossible to ignore.

**Layout Paradigm:**
Broken grid with intentional asymmetry. Large, bold typography breaks out of containers. Text blocks overlap images slightly. Sections have dramatically different widths - some full-bleed, others constrained to 40% of the viewport. Vertical rhythm is disrupted intentionally to create visual tension and maintain attention.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Oversized, bold typography with tight letter-spacing that demands attention
2. Raw, unprocessed photography with high contrast and grain texture
3. Exposed borders and dividers that look hand-drawn or stamped, suggesting DIY authenticity

**Interaction Philosophy:**
Interactions are immediate and honest. No smooth transitions - elements snap into place. Hover states are bold (full color inversions, scale jumps). Scrolling reveals content with hard cuts rather than fades. The experience should feel direct and unmediated.

**Animation:**
Minimal animation, and when present, it's abrupt and purposeful. Elements slide in from edges with linear easing (no curves). Clicks trigger instant feedback with no delay. Page transitions are hard cuts. The only smooth animation: a slow, continuous parallax on hero images to suggest depth.

**Typography System:**
- Display: Space Grotesk (geometric, bold, slightly industrial)
- Body: IBM Plex Mono (monospace for technical credibility and brutalist aesthetic)
- Accent: Space Grotesk Bold Condensed (for labels and CTAs)
Hierarchy: Massive display (72-120px), bold subheads (32-48px), regular body (16px monospace), tiny labels (12px uppercase)
</text>

**Design Movement:** Neo-Botanical Maximalism

**Core Principles:**
1. Abundant visual richness through layered botanical illustrations, textures, and organic patterns
2. Celebration of natural ingredients via lush, detailed plant imagery and earthy material references
3. Confident femininity that embraces curves, softness, and decorative elements without apology
4. Generous information density where every screen is full of discoverable details and visual interest

**Color Philosophy:**
A vibrant, saturated palette inspired by botanical gardens and apothecary traditions. Deep forest green as the primary, complemented by golden yellow (calendula), deep plum (elderberry), coral pink (rose hip), and cream (shea butter). Each color represents a natural ingredient category. Gradients flow between colors like watercolor washes. The overall effect is lush, alive, and unapologetically colorful.

**Layout Paradigm:**
Organic, flowing layouts that resist rigid grids. Content follows curved paths and diagonal lines. Sections nest within each other like petals or leaves. Overlapping layers create depth. Text wraps around illustrated elements. The layout should feel like navigating through a garden or flipping through an illuminated manuscript.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Hand-drawn botanical illustrations that frame content areas and serve as section dividers
2. Textured backgrounds suggesting paper, fabric, or natural materials (subtle linen, watercolor washes)
3. Ornamental borders and decorative flourishes that echo vintage apothecary labels

**Interaction Philosophy:**
Interactions are delightful and rewarding. Hover states bloom (scale up with rotation). Scrolling reveals illustrated elements that grow or animate subtly. Ingredient cards flip to reveal detailed information. The experience should feel like exploring a treasure box or opening a beautifully packaged gift.

**Animation:**
Rich, playful animations with elastic easing. Elements bounce gently into view. Botanical illustrations grow from stems. Page transitions use organic wipes (like leaves sliding across). Micro-interactions include petal-like particles on button clicks. Timing is slightly slower (400-600ms) to feel luxurious.

**Typography System:**
- Display: Playfair Display (elegant serif with high contrast, suggesting luxury and tradition)
- Body: Lora (readable serif with warmth and personality)
- Accent/Labels: Montserrat (clean sans for ingredient lists and modern contrast)
Hierarchy: Elegant display (56-80px with generous line-height), graceful subheads (28-36px), comfortable body (17-19px), delicate labels (13px)
</text>
